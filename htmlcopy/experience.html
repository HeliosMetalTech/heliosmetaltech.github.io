About Us

Founded in 2012, Helios Metal-Tech is company based in the GTA, encompassing all your needs for architectural stainless steel and metal products. Helios Metal-Tech is supported by a team of licensed mechanical & structural engineers, and technicians, with many years of experience in the field.
