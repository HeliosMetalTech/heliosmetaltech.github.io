Design & Consultation

Helios Metal-Tech is endorsed by a team of mechanical, structural engineers, and technicians, with over 20 years in the field. Drawings are done with the highest precision. No matter what size or extent your needs are, you will be satisfied with consulting our cooperative team. We will use our expertise in helping you towards the development of a long lasting, and efficient solution.


Fabrication

From architectural stainless steel parts to custom templates, we can build it! With the utilization of advanced software, metals parts can be fabricated accurate to the millimeter following government regulations, the building code & your design requirements. Orders are always made on time, and with care. Check out our gallery for pictures of completed projects and products, including railing, architectural frames, ladders, and piping support.


Installation
